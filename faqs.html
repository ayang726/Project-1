Keep In Mind💡:

  • 👀
    Make sure the child is visually attending to the video demonstration and webcam video.
  • 🙌
    Have the child start and end with “ready hands” flat on the desk.
  • 🙋🏻‍♀️
    Do not give specific verbal prompts
    (for example: say “Do this!”, do not say” Touch head!”).
  • 🗝
    Assist the child as much as needed – success is key!
  • 🏀
    Hold the exercise demand until successful completion (even if full assistance is required).
  • 🎠
    Allow frequent breaks, provide positive re-enforcement,
    and click the COMPLETE button after each exercise completion!
